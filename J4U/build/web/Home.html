Frequently Asked Questions

Clarity before you commit.

Is my data private and secure?

Yes. All communication and documents utilize 256-bit encryption. Your data is never sold to third parties.

Can interns see my sensitive files?

No. We employ strict Role-Based Access Control (RBAC). Interns only see redacted or strictly assigned materials approved by the lead lawyer.

How are lawyers verified?

Every advocate must upload their official Bar Council ID during registration. A human admin manually verifies these credentials before the profile goes live.

What if my lawyer becomes inactive?

Our platform tracks milestones. If a case stagnates, you can flag it for admin review or initiate a secure transfer of your digital vault to a new advocate.

Is Justice4U recognized by the courts?

Justice4U is an independent, secure SaaS collaboration tool for legal professionals and clients, not a government entity. It aids preparation, not official court scheduling.

Is this platform compliant with Indian IT laws?

Yes, our infrastructure is designed to align with data protection standards outlined in the Information Technology Act, 2000.

Is the platform free to use?

During our Beta phase, core collaborative features are free. Future phases will introduce tiered premium features for law firms.

Can I switch lawyers mid-case?

Yes. Because your evidence is stored in your personal encrypted vault, you retain ownership of your files and can revoke/grant access as needed.

Designed for the Indian Legal Ecosystem

Justice4U strictly respects Advocate-Client Confidentiality. Our platform infrastructure is built aligning with the Information Technology Act, 2000, ensuring the secure handling of legal documentation, ethical intern supervision, and rigorous manual human verification.
