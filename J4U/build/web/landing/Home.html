The Justice4U Ecosystem

For Citizens

Submit your legal requirements instantly. Track your case progress in real-time, view uploaded documents, and communicate seamlessly with your assigned advocate.

For Legal Counsel

Manage your entire client docket efficiently. Review incoming requests, organize your schedule, and assign crucial research tasks to interns from one central hub.

For Legal Interns

Streamlined Workflow

Platform Mechanics

01

Case Initiation

Clients register on the portal and submit a description of their legal requirement, automatically routing it to the appropriate domain.

02
Counsel Assignment

Verified lawyers review the pending docket and accept cases that match their expertise, establishing a direct connection with the client.

03
Collaborative Action

Lawyers can draft interns to assist with active research. The client stays updated via their dashboard until the matter reaches resolution.
